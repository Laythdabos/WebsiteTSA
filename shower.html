The Resource Saving Shower Head

Low-flow shower heads have a flow rate lower than 1.5 gpm (gallons per minute), while traditional shower heads use 2.5 gpm. Heating up water takes up energy. Thus, users are also cutting down on their energy expenditure by cutting down on water consumption. Low-flow shower heads can decrease water consumption by 40% or more
