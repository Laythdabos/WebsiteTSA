    Energy Efficient Ceiling Fan

    Energy-efficient fans are important because they save up to 50 percent more energy than standard ceiling fans. For example, regular fans are conventional ceiling fans built with an AC Induction Motor (ACIM). Typically, they consume around 75W. Some old fans may consume up to 90W. On the contrary, Energy-efficient fans or power-saving ceiling fans use a minimum level of electronic technology to reduce power consumption. They are BEE (Bureau of Energy Efficiency) 5-star rated fans and consume only 50W, and Super energy efficient fans only consume 35W.
