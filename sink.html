Low Flow Sinks

The sink in the bathroom is where people often spend the majority of their time. The sink is responsible for almost all the water usage. From the faucet to the drain, thousands, if not millions, of gallons of water are circulating through your bathroom sink every year. But, the sink is a major problem in wasting water. Finding a low-flow sink could result in the lowering of this phenomenon. Also, repairing appliances that are wasting water is the first step in conserving energy.
