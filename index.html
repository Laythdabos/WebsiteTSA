    Ridge For Green Energy

    This website is designed to give future homeowners a one-stop shop to make an energy-efficient home. Click the navigation buttons on top to view the different rooms you can reduce carbon for.






    Function of the Website

     Our goal is to reduce energy consumption and environmental impacts. This website is intended for all homeowners to obtain their energy efficient appliances. We as homeowners are responsible for the metric tons of CO2 emissions that are accelerating the greenhouse gas effect. This is the same greenhouse gas effect that is destroying our planet. Quick and affordable switches to clean energy from LED light bulbs to low flush toilets, there is an alternative for everyone to switch to. With energy prices and CO2 emissions skyrocketing, it will be in the homeowners' best interest to switch to more energy efficient appliances. In 2021 alone, the U.S. produced 6,340 million metric tons of carbon dioxide equivalents. This was a year when covid had decreased many social outings, meaning it is likely even higher now that regulations have been lifted.
